About Me

My name is Samer Khiari, I am a freelance audio engineer and sound designer based in Tunisia.My work consists of recording, mixing, mastering, and editing sound, as well as creating new and interesting soundscapes.I studied and obtained a bachelor degree in cinema and audiovisual and additionally have a diploma in audio engineering.In this portfolio you can find a range of projects I have been working on, from music, to film and also my works as a sound artist.
